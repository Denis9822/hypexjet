ПОЛИТИКА ДЛЯ НЕСОВЕРШЕННОЛЕТНИХ

Last updated: 07.09.2022

 

HypeXJet N.V. is the owner company of the games and services of the website https://hypexjet.com. The company is registered under number: 161322, and under the address: Zuikertuintjeweg ZIN (Zuikertuin Tower), Willemstad, Curaçao.

 

The Games and Services provided by HypeXJet N.V. are intended for people over 18 years old. Anyone underage of 18 is restricted to access the gaming platform by the law, and furthermore, is not allowed to create an account nor play any of the games. 

 

The company follows the rules and the regulations in order to prevent children from gambling. To do so HypeXJet N.V., implemented a system that checks the age of the users through uploaded documents. That is, the company has the right to request any type of document that can confirm the age of the client. Anyone willing to use the services provided by HypeXJet N.V., is responsible to commit to our rules and regulations and understand the importance of it. 

 

In case if the company is unable to check the age of the user, it has the right to temporarily close the account up until the necessary documents are uploaded for the review. Additionally, if the documents are not received after all, and the company is not able to verify the age of the user, the HypeXJet N.V. has the right to refuse to provide any services, and completely remove/block the account of the client. 

 

The act of violation of the rules and regulations of the HypeXJet N.V., regarding the underage gaming/gambling, will lead to: 

  • Suspension of the account,
  • Loss of the user’s progress in the platform,
  • The player will be obligated to return the amount of money that he/she has previously withdrawn from the account,
  • The funds deposited into the account will be returned only to the legal representative of the underage player,
  • All the transactions will be invalidated. 

Any adult user of the gaming platform, who shares the same computer with an underage, should prevent the child from accessing the services provided by the HypeXJet N.V.. 

Our company recommends installing filtering software on the computer, for the purpose of restricting the access to the platform to an underage child. Finally, please, do not save the password, as this reduces the chance that any child may get the access into the website.
